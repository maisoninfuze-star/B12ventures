1. Who We Are

B12 Ventures ("we," "us," "our") is a digital growth and AI solutions agency operating in Laval, Québec, Canada. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit b12ventures.com (the "Site"), submit our forms, or use our services. It is drafted to comply with Québec's Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector as modernized by Law 25, the federal Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), and Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL).

Person in Charge of the Protection of Personal Information

In accordance with Law 25, the person in charge of the protection of personal information at B12 Ventures is the Privacy Officer, B12 Ventures.
Email: contact@b12ventures.vip · Phone: +1 (778) 802-4486

2. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide — when you submit our contact form: first and last name, email address, phone number, service of interest, and the content of your message. When you opt in to text messaging: your mobile number and your consent choices.
  • Information collected automatically — basic technical data needed to serve the Site (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited). The Site does not use advertising trackers.
  • Client project information — information reasonably required to deliver contracted services.

3. Why We Collect It (Purposes)

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes;
  • To deliver, manage, and invoice our services;
  • To send appointment confirmations, reminders, and service updates;
  • To send marketing communications only where you have expressly consented;
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We collect only the personal information necessary for these purposes, and we identify our purposes at or before the time of collection.

4. Consent

We rely on your express consent, given freely and in a clear, granular way (for example, separate unchecked boxes for marketing and non-marketing text messages on our forms). Consent to receive messages is never a condition of purchase. You may withdraw your consent at any time — by texting STOP to any of our messages, clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email, or contacting our Privacy Officer. Commercial electronic messages we send comply with CASL: they identify us, provide our contact information, and include a functioning unsubscribe mechanism.

5. Mobile Information & Text Messaging (SMS)

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Your SMS opt-in consent and mobile number are used solely to operate our text messaging program as described in our Terms & Conditions (message frequency varies; message and data rates may apply; text HELP for help; text STOP to unsubscribe).

6. Sharing & Service Providers

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. We share personal information only with service providers who process it on our behalf, under contractual protections, to operate the Site and our business:

  • Form processing — our contact form submissions are transmitted to us by a form-delivery provider;
  • CRM & communications platform — to manage inquiries, appointments, email and SMS communications;
  • Website hosting — to serve this Site;
  • Professional advisors or authorities — where required by law.

Communication outside Québec: Some of our service providers store or process information on servers located outside Québec and outside Canada (notably in the United States). Before communicating personal information outside Québec, we conduct the assessment required by Law 25 and use contractual safeguards so that your information receives adequate protection. By submitting your information, you are informed that it may be communicated outside Québec for the purposes described above.

7. Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes above or as required by law (for example, accounting records). When information is no longer required, we destroy or anonymize it securely. SMS consent records are retained as proof of consent for as long as required by messaging regulations.

8. Security

We apply reasonable safeguards proportionate to the sensitivity of the information — including encrypted transmission (HTTPS), access limited to those who need it, and reputable providers. In the event of a confidentiality incident involving your personal information that presents a risk of serious injury, we will notify the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec (CAI) and the affected persons, as required by Law 25.

9. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you;
  • Rectify inaccurate, incomplete, or ambiguous information;
  • Withdraw your consent to the use or communication of your information;
  • Request cessation of dissemination or de-indexing where dissemination contravenes the law or a court order;
  • Be informed of exclusively automated decisions made about you, where applicable;
  • File a complaint with our Privacy Officer, and with the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec (cai.gouv.qc.ca) or the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Privacy Officer at contact@b12ventures.vip. We respond within 30 days.

10. Cookies & Similar Technologies

The Site uses only the technical means necessary to display its content (such as web fonts and embedded media). We do not use advertising cookies. If we add analytics or other tracking technologies in the future, we will update this Policy and, where required, request your consent and provide deactivation options in accordance with Law 25.

11. Children

Our Site and services are intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors under 14. If you believe a minor has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates the latest revision. Material changes will be posted prominently on this page.
